Understanding Institute

Auto-Syncing Stripe & Google Calendar System

A bulletproof scheduling and transactional automation platform built for the Understanding Institute. The administrative team simply schedules and updates events directly inside Google Calendar; the system autonomously tracks changes, processes client Stripe transactions, orchestrates recurring events, generates Google Meet ('glink') sessions, and triggers instant automated reminder emails for attendees and hosts, including self-service rescheduling and cancellations.

  • Zero-Admin Calendar Sync: The institute's staff administers everything by modifying their Google Calendar. The engine instantly detects new bookings, cancellations, and reschedules with zero web portal logins.
  • Autonomous Stripe Checkout: Seamless card processing with automatic webhook reconciliation that handles refunds for cancellations, registration payouts, and invoices completely hands-free.
  • Google Meet 'glink' Integration: Instantly creates unique online conference channels, links them directly within calendar invites, and attaches them to booking confirmation notifications.
  • Reminders & Cancel Interfaces: Client-side self-service links in automated notifications empower users to cancel or reschedule, with the engine executing downstream calendar and email adjustments automatically.
