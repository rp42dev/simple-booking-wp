Simple Booking

Enterprise WordPress Booking Engine

A lightweight, highly resilient, and modular scheduling orchestrator for WordPress. Integrates secure payment gateways, dual-calendar ecosystems, and automated virtual meeting utilities natively with zero third-party platform lock-in.

  • Smart Payments: Seamless Stripe Checkout integration supporting both paid reservations and instant free bookings.
  • Dual-Calendar Sync: Automated, real-time bi-directional synchronization via Google Calendar and Microsoft Graph APIs.
  • Fault-Tolerant Webhooks: An advanced background webhook retry queue guaranteeing data and booking integrity.
  • Secure Management: Clean Customer Dashboard with tokenized URL links for secure, code-free reschedules and cancellations.
